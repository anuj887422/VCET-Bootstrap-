Applied Sciences & Humanities


The departments of Applied Mathematics, Applied Physics, Applied Chemistry and Communication Skills came into existence with the inception of the college in June 1994. These departments were later merged into Humanities Department and, then in 2002, into Department of Applied Sciences and Humanities. Currently the department holds the subjects Applied Physics, Applied Chemistry, Applied Mathematics and Communication Skills and Business Communication & Ethics.

The department is now fully flourished as a strong department with dedicated teachers that support all other engineering departments in the college.

The staff members do attend refresher/orientation courses, whenever the schedule allows. Few seminars have been conducted by the department for students & staff on different topics. Counselling sessions for F.E. (First year of Engineering course) students are conducted by all F.E. faculties in the department so that they settle comfortably in their first year at this college. The ‘F.E. in-charge-ship’ (administrative head-ship for F.E.) has always been successfully managed by the faculty of the Department of Applied Sciences & Humanities.

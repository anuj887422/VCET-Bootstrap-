CIVIL ENGINEERING


A warm welcome to the Civil Engineering department. The department of the Civil engineering is established in the 2013 in the Vidyavardhini’s College of Engineering and Technology. The Civil Engineering department aims a topmost Institution by generating professional with higher degree of technical knowledge, proficient skills and principled values. The Department of Civil Engineering along with its multi-layered faculty sustains its robust relations with the industry and other institutes by organizing various events such as Product Showcase and Vidyavardhini’s National Project Showcase (VNPS). The students are always invigorated to participate extra-curricular and co-curricular events which are essential for the building a team spirit and development of administrative skills results in their personality development. I believe that the students of the department would rationalize the reliability of the department by presenting a extraordinary level of proficient ability in their corresponding job areas.


HOD OF CIVIL DEPARTMENT
Prof. Dipak Choudhari

VISION AND MISSION

  • Vision

    The Department of Civil Engineering committed to provide distinctive educational, research and community services prospects for students and faculty to build our nation.

  • Mission

      We at VCET aim
    1. To serve civil engineers of great quality, technical skills and ethically as well as morally strong to build our society and develop humanity.
    2. To develop the department as a center of brilliance in the field of civil engineering and associated with research and development.
    3. To offer practical base and quality consultancy services to the community to solve civil engineering problems.
    4. To encourage innovation and unique intelligent in the minds of students to serves the solution for the current and future challenges of our society.
POs,PEOs,PSOs

  • Program Outcomes (POs):

    1. An ability to apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and Engineering.
    2. An ability to design and conduct experiments, as well as to analyze and interpret data.
    3. An ability to design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within realistic constraints.
    4. An ability to design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within realistic constraints.
    5. An ability to use the techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for civil engineering practice.
    6. Knowledge of contemporary issues.
    7. The broad education necessary to understand the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and societal context.
    8. An understanding of professional and ethical responsibility
    9. An ability to function in multidisciplinary teams.
    10. An ability to communicate effectively.
    11. Recognition of the need for, and an ability to engage in life-long learning
    12. An understanding of engineering and management principles and apply these to one’s own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects.

  • Program Educational Objectives (PEOs):

    1. Graduates will have effective profession in the area of Civil Engineering.
    2. Graduates will satisfy the need of society by providing professional and ethical practices.
    3. Graduates will pursue higher studies in the field of Civil Engineering and its applications.

  • Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs):

    1. Proficiency in Civil Engineering: Apply knowledge of mathematics and physical science with higher proficiency in the core areas of civil engineering fields.
    2. Analytical and Executive Skill: Develop a plan, drawings, analyze, design, write specification, prepare cost estimates and perform all kinds of civil engineering activities.
    3. Application of Technology at the frontiers of knowledge: Encourage for the application of modern engineering tools and multidisciplinary concept to solve civil engineering problems and research.
    4. Responsibility: Provide sustainable solutions to complex civil engineering problems for the development of nation and society.
